NDVI stands for Normalised Difference Vegetation Index. This is useful for measuring the health of plants! A healthy plant will reflect a lot of infrared light.



Filters can be used on cameras to prevent some wavelengths of light from reaching the sensor. For instance, most digital cameras have an infrared filter to stop infrared light from reaching the sensor. We can convert an image taken with a modified camera so that it can be used to measure NDVI.
